SwarmGen / CIS 589 / 2026

Stable diffusion,
across edges.

SD-Turbo partitioned across three heterogeneous edge devices. CLIP runs on a CPU laptop, UNet on an RTX 5060, VAE on a Raspberry Pi 4B. The Pi cannot fit the full pipeline on its own; the swarm makes it a participant. Heartbeat-driven fault tolerance reroutes the VAE stage to the GPU when the Pi dies mid-flight.
